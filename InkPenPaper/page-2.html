Comics To 'Heavy' Literature

Reading isn't just for bookish people. It's for everyone who might pass time during commuting, reading a detective or perhaps historical fiction during a holiday. A comic book isn't less than a novel. Non-fiction is as good a read as fiction. You can't do reading wrong. Knowing your abc is all you need. Just open a page and start reading. Enjoy.
